NO HEAT?
What To Do If Your Apartment Doesn't Have Heat or Water:

Winter is right around the corner and earlier this year from Oct. 1 to Jan. 16, HPD says it received more than 61,000 separate complaints about lack of heat. of complaint were reported. Here is what to do in case you are lacking heat in your home.

Season

Heat season runs from Oct. 1 to May 31 in New York City. Landlords must also provide hot water at a consistent temperature of at least 120 degrees Fahrenheit, 365 days a year.

The Law

Hot water must be provided 365 days per year at a constant minimum temperature of 120 degrees Fahrenheit. During heat season, property owners must follow rules about how warm it is in their buildings.

During The Day

Between the hours of 6:00am and 10:00pm, if the outside temperature falls below 55 degrees, the inside temperature is required to be at least 68 degrees Fahrenheit.

During The Night

Between the hours of 10:00pm and 6:00am, the inside temperature is required to be at least 62 degrees Fahrenheit.

Keep A Record & Notify Your Landlord

Take down the temperature of the apartment, here is a hanfy link to keep track of the temperature and make sure to contact your landlord via phone, email or both. Make note of how many times you have contacted him.

NO ANSWER?

If you have contacted your landlord and there is no answer, you can file a complaing by calling 311.

CALL 311

You can make an anonymous complaint about a maintenance problem that is affecting an entire residential building. When reporting a problem in an apartment, you must provide your contact information.

If a tenant files a 311 complaint related to heat or hot water, HPD attempts to notify the building owner or managing agent and may also attempt to contact the tenant to see whether service has been restored. If service has not been restored, an HPD inspector will go to the building to verify the complaint and issue the appropriate violation.

NYCHA RESIDENTS

If you are a NYCHA resident you can report a heat or hot water problem to NYCHA's Customer Contact Center 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year by calling (718)-707-7771.
